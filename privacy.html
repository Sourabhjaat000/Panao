Last Updated: January 1, 2023

Key Point: All photo processing happens in your browser. We do not store, upload, or access your images. Your photos remain 100% private on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how PanCardPhotoResizer.in ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our online PAN card photo resizing tool (the "Service").

Our Service is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not upload your photos to any server - all processing occurs locally in your web browser

  • We do not use tracking cookies for analytics or advertising

For operational purposes, we may collect minimal anonymous data:

3. How We Use Information

Any collected data is used solely to:

  • Analyze general usage patterns
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect standard log data:

  • Web hosting provider: For serving our website files

  • Font providers: Google Fonts for typography

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our Service uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: For basic functionality if implemented
  • No advertising cookies: We do not display ads or use tracking for marketing

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect any collected information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Regular security audits of our infrastructure
  • Minimal data retention policies

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
